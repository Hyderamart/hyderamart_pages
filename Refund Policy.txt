Refund Policy
Hyderamart.in Application

Last Updated: June 20, 2025

1. Introduction
This Refund Policy outlines how Hyderamart.in processes refunds for purchases made through our application. We are committed to ensuring a transparent and fair refund process for all our customers. This policy covers all products and services purchased through the Hyderamart.in platform.

2. Eligibility for Refunds
Refunds may be issued under the following circumstances:

Wrong item delivered that doesn't match your order
Products substantially damaged or deteriorated at time of delivery
Undelivered orders due to technical errors on our platform
Orders canceled before acceptance by Hyderamart
Orders canceled by Hyderamart due to unavailability or other valid reasons
Important Conditions:
Refund requests must be initiated on the same day of delivery. Products must be in their original sealed packaging, unopened and unused. No refunds will be processed for products that have been opened, used, or consumed either partially or completely.

3. Non-Refundable Items
The following items and situations are not eligible for refunds:

Perishable goods that have been delivered in good condition
Products with opened packaging or signs of use
Customized or personalized items
Digital products that have been downloaded or accessed
Orders canceled after acceptance by Hyderamart
4. Refund Request Process
To request a refund, please follow these steps:

1
Initiate Request
Contact our customer support within 24 hours of delivery through the app or at support@hyderamart.in

2
Provide Evidence
Submit required proof including images of damaged/wrong products with visible issues

3
Verification
Our team will verify your request within 48 business hours

4
Refund Processing
Once approved, refund will be initiated to your original payment method

Important: Please do not dispose of any products until you receive confirmation from our support team. In some cases, we may require you to return the products to complete the refund process.

5. Refund Processing Time
Refund requests are typically processed within 7 business days after verification
Once processed, refunds may take additional time to appear in your account based on your financial institution's processing time
You will receive email confirmation when your refund has been processed
6. Method of Refund
Refunds will be issued using the original payment method:

Credit/Debit Card payments: Refunded to the original card
Digital Wallets: Credited back to your wallet account
UPI Payments: Refunded to the original UPI ID
Cash on Delivery: Refunded via bank transfer to your registered account
7. Exceptions and Limitations
Hyderamart is not responsible for payments that fail due to insufficient funds, incorrect payment details, or expired cards
No refunds will be issued for orders delayed due to incorrect or incomplete address information
Special promotions or clearance items may have different refund policies as stated at time of purchase
8. Contact Us
If you have any questions about our refund policy or need assistance with a refund request, please contact our support team:

support@hyderamart.in
Our customer support team is available Monday-Saturday, 9 AM to 6 PM IST.
